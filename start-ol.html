OPENLIFE

Core Vision

A group focused on equipping Gods people to a live a Gospel centred life together

Team of 2 committed leaders and 4 committed group members

Team leaders need to worship regularly at established church

Groups to meet weekly with seasonal breaks if needed

Mission: Heal, Equip, Release and Celebrate

 

Phases have been implemented into this group to ensure that it is truly God’s will for a certain Open Life group to go ahead and/or continue. Each phase is designed to test if God is working amongst the group. If a group does not meet the expectations of each phase it will go under review which is explained below. There will be grace used in the case of a review.

Phase 1

Two leaders enter training and agree to all open ministries confessions and cultures. Be affirmed that this is their call and gift set by church leadership.

Phase 2

Find 4 committed participants who are willing enter into an Open Life group.

Phase 3

After 6 months if group has not increased or has decreased in number – group to be reviewed by church leadership.

Phase 4

After 12 months if group has not embraced a non-Christian within your group life - group to be reviewed by church leadership.

Split

When a group exceeds 12, groups will release 2 new leaders to seek 4 new people to start a new OpenLife group.

At any stage if a member or leader of an open life group feels the call of God they may join or start a core team for an Open House group.

Review

At a review of a group, church leadership will either refocus or dissolve a group.

If it is decided to dissolve the group in that instance members of group are strongly encouraged to connect into other Open Life groups.
