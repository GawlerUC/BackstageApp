OPENLIFE

Read

Read a Scripture passage.

Ask someone to repeat the passage in their own words. When they are done, ask the group if the person left anything important out of the story.

 

Ask

What does this story tell us about God?

What does this story tell us about humankind?

What does this story tell us about God’s plan?

Give them time to answer. Resist the urge to tell them what the story means. If they aren’t getting it, read additional passages of Scripture or ask more questions.

If this story is true, how does that change how we act? If lived out how will it change the world?

What questions do you have about this story?

Do you know anyone who needs to hear this story?

Encourage them share the story with anyone they name.
